ImratGroup Meets Modern Global Trends in Green Energy

ImratGroup keeps pace with global trends that can change the world for the better. We are seeing more and more countries passing laws related to the increase in green energy.

In the EU, we can see how they are investing heavily in renewables and already, according to data, wind farms have contributed more energy to the EU than conventional gas-fired power plants.

The EU's energy sector is in the middle of a monumental shift,’ says Sarah Brown, European Programme Director. ‘Fossil fuels are playing a smaller role than ever as a wind and solar system becomes more and more apparent

That is why IG is opening a new branch of investment, which is designed for a specific sector and can benefit both ImratGroup and the people of the world.

IG Future Energy: Empowering Asia and Africa with Renewable Energy

IG Future Energy is a trust fund that aims to provide the regions of Asia and Africa with more renewable energy sources, with a view to utilising them both domestically and in export markets. Global demand for electricity is only going to increase in the coming decades, as we see technology companies switching to low carbon footprint components and leading countries actively subsidising and sponsoring companies that promote green energy. In this way IG will be able to raise the living standards of low-income regions in the shortest possible time and earn high dividends, as it will always have an ownership stake in the green energy sources built.
