Imrat Group's innovation areas

Imrat Group is a leading company that covers areas such as the development and application of artificial intelligence in the fields of medicine, energy, and blockchain. Founded in 2021 by a team of highly skilled financial management investment professionals, Imrat Group combines reliability, cutting-edge ideas and transparency to generate stable income and grow the company.

The Imrat Group team continuously invests in various energy, healthcare and blockchain startups to contribute to the development of the world and its technology. In addition, our team develops proprietary software to analyse market data. Our team believes that Imrat Group's high performance is based on discipline, highly educated team, common goals and technological advancement.

Imrat Group - reliable, practical, modernity

  • The company's core business is supporting the financing of AI, medical solutions and green energy companies. The majority of our income comes from dividends from invested companies, often selling their developments to large corporations or providing subscription-based services. In addition to being profitable, these companies also allow Imrat Group, as the main financial donor, to keep up to date with their latest developments and apply their cutting-edge solutions to other areas, allowing them to combine different projects to increase productivity or to provide an alternative approach to solving a problem.

  • Imrat Group's secondary activity lies in the field of blockchain. This area brings together traders, cryptography specialists and L1-L3 solutions. The digital department is multi-purpose and includes long-term trading of digital assets, building bots to analyse data, sourcing and investing in projects that provide a secure, fast and low-carbon blockchain solution. The digital department provides constant returns as well as market summary: trends, large capital flows, forecasting the development of an area based on the investment of funds.

  • Thanks to the coordinated work of the digital department and their reports, Imrat Group has an understanding of global trends, allowing it to invest in startups at their nascent stage and earn high returns when they become large companies or are acquired by other corporations.

  • Thanks to the directions of our work, investors in companies have a reliable investment partner in the Imrat Group. Our team emphasises three main areas in its activities, ensuring high returns for both the company and investors. ImratGroup allows convenient tracking and management of invested funds, as users can access all necessary information in their personal investor cabinet. We have abandoned the format of quarterly portfolio results, as is common for others, in favour of increasing the reliability and transparency of our work.
