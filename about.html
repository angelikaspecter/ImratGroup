Imrat Group focuses on AI, medicine, green energy, and blockchain for reducing carbon footprints and advancing digital assets.

Mission of Imrat Group of Companies:
The core mission of our company is to multiply capital by investing in cutting-edge startups and blockchain solutions.

Over the past two years, our team has been consistently following this mission and proving the success of our chosen direction.

Why Imrat Group?

We actively work to minimise costs and maximise profits with AI solutions, so IG provides unique opportunities to grow your money by investing in reliable startups.

Your returns will be made up of dividends from startups, income that is earned from IPOs, and from digital assets and work with them. Dividends are generated from the startups' profits: when we invest, we sign contracts stating that a portion of the profits of the company in which we have invested goes to our official accounts at ImratGroup. The investment income is then distributed to all participants according to their contribution shares.

ImratGroup Values

  • Empowerment

    Our company empowers everyone to take control of their financial future and realise their full potential in investments, micro-investments and start-ups.

  • Growth

    Our commitment to a culture of continuous growth and development helps us create an environment where improvement is key for our team. Continuous learning, mutual support, encouragement and development are the pillars on which ImratGroup stands.

  • Unity

    Our philosophy is based on the belief that no matter how much a company is worth or earns, the most important aspect is its people. It is the people who bring the most value to a company, which is reflected in its products, capabilities and services. We strive to bring together people who are committed to being one with ImratGroup and sharing in our success.

  • Support

    Our company offers reliable support and guidance, helping people navigate the complex landscape of investments, micro-investments and startups throughout their journey.

  • Our Advantages

    Our employees are constantly engaged in intellectual and analytical work related to the search for various startups that can provide long-term and high returns.

    For this purpose, we have set up several internal departments that analyse market data on sectors such as the development and application of artificial intelligence, medicine, green energy, and blockchain. The fulcrum of all our solutions is the presence of AI applications at work, as AI has much greater computational, labour, analytical capabilities than humans.

    By giving an edge to startup solutions that seek to move away from the human element, we ensure a safe and secure collaboration that is less dependent on political, corruption and other risks.

  • Accessibility, Convenience, Transparency

    In developing our system, we have endeavoured to make it accessible to anyone interested in investing in various projects. We created a user-friendly interface that allows investors to intuitively find the information they need. Our main focus was on transparency, allowing users to track all their returns and transactions across their investment portfolios.

    At ImratGroup, we are committed to continuously improving our company to meet the growing needs of our users. We value your feedback and encourage you to share any suggestions for improvement.

    Please contact us at ImratGroupSupport@gmail.com.
